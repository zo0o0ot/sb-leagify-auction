AUCTION RULES & HELP

payments

CAPITAL & BUDGET

Each coach starts the draft session with a bankroll of $200.00. This budget is static and does not increase. Whole-dollar bidding rules apply: fractional cents are not permitted.

gavel

BIDDING PROTOCOL

  • Minimum Threshold

    The starting bid for any school must be at least $1.

  • Whole Dollar Exchange

    All transactions must occur in increments of $1.00. "Cents" or partial currency units are strictly prohibited.

leaderboard

SCORING PROTOCOL

1st Round Selection = 25pts
2nd-3rd Round = 10pts
Day 3 Selection = 5pts
